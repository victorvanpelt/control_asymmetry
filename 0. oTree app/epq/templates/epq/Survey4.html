In this task, we ask you to imagine that you have been randomly paired with another person whom we refer to as the 'Other'.
You do not know and will not knowingly meet the 'Other' person in the future.
Both you and the ‘Other’ person will be making choices by choosing either the letter A, B, or C.
Your own choices will produce fictional points for both yourself and the ‘Other’ person.
Likewise, the 'Other’s' choice will produce fictional points for him/her and for you.
Every point has value: the more fictional points you receive, the better for you, and the more fictional points the ‘Other’ receives, the better for him/her.

Before you make your choices, please keep in mind that there are no right or wrong answers.
That is, choose the option that you, for whatever reason, prefer most.
For each of the nine situations, choose A, B, or C depending on which you prefer most.
